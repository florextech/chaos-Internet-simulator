# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Chaos Internet Simulator to be an open, respectful, and inclusive community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in communication.
- Focus feedback on ideas and implementation, not people.
- Assume positive intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Share constructive review comments.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing or sharing private information.
- Disruptive trolling or repeated bad-faith interactions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation for violations.

## Reporting

Report concerns privately to maintainers with context and links/screenshots where relevant.
